Potential improvement

In order to truly realize the "elderly care" for China's empty nesters, we still need to make the following efforts:


1. Expand the supply of elderly services

We have to reduce the gap between the coverage and quality of urban and rural elderly services.


2. Further improve the support system for elderly health

Increase financial investment to increase the supply of resources for elderly health services and make the allocation of resources more reasonable. Actively carry out home medical services such as family hospital beds and home visits to further meet the health needs of the elderly.


3. Integrate a variety of industries into senior care services and innovative senior care services

For example, services such as education and training for the elderly, cultural tourism, fitness and leisure, financial support, etc., and growing the elderly products industry around the clothing, food, housing and transportation of the elderly and rehabilitation care. And focus on strengthening the ability of technological innovation to provide more intelligent products and services for the elderly.


4. Improve the regulatory system for elderly services and provide a sound system

For example, we can use long-term care insurance to make elderly services more complete.


5. The social environment should become more suitable for the elderly to live in

National model senior-friendly communities should be comprehensively promoted to make the atmosphere of respecting, loving and helping the elderly stronger in the whole society, and to strengthen the participation of the elderly in the society to help them better integrate into today's society.
